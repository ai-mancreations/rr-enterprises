Frequently asked

Straight answers before you call.

Which areas do you serve?+

We're based in Kakinada and serve clients across East Godavari and the wider Andhra Pradesh region for interiors, solar, and construction projects.

Do you handle the solar subsidy paperwork?+

Yes — RR Solar Energies handles PM Surya Ghar subsidy applications and net-metering paperwork as part of residential installations.

Can I use more than one division for the same project?+

Yes, this is common — for example a new home build with RR Homes Construction, finished by RR Interiors, with rooftop solar added by RR.

How do I get a quote?+

Fill out the enquiry form below, message us on WhatsApp, or call us directly at 8367086748. We'll arrange a site visit before preparing an itemised estimate.
